What is Havenir?

We are a Global Provider of Global IT and Enterprise Application Solution which includes Business Analytic Dashboards, ERP Solutions, Custom Development, Systems Integration and Technical Services.

How can we help?

Our goal is to help integrate as many platforms as possible with ERP, whether it be IoT, Industrial Machines, HR Management devices and many more. We want to provide companies with a one-stop solution to all their business needs in order to enable them to achieve maximum growth.

Gaining your trust?

We are an enthusiastic and a professional team of confident individuals with the right experience to handle complex tasks/projects. We have satisfied clients all over the world including Europe, Middle east, South Asia and Kingdom of Saudi Aribia who have achieved their potential through Havenir Solutions' services.

CEO Message

I have always had a keen fascination with how businesses are run and what their relation is to the technologies they use along the way. With hundreds of applications available across storefronts, it can be quite difficult to understand which ones you need.

I wanted to address this issue, as I am a firm believer that all company matters should be managed in a single holistic place. The beauty of ERP systems is that they are not confined to one specific domain. They can be personalized as per your business needs. We here at Havenir Solutions are trying to bridge the gap and provide our clients with a smooth and effective implementation journey.

We already have solutions for several domains that include accounting, human resources, manufacturing, and even website development. If you fall in a different category, then give us a call and we would love to take this challenge and design new solutions for you.

Havenir CSR

We aim to empower everyone to understand the better use of software technology. Software is very commonly used however, many companies fail to understand the effective use of these solutions. One of the biggest examples is that of an ERP system, although ERP provides all solutions under one roof but organizations today are still using multiple softwares which increases the cost and reduces efficiency.
Our program has the following objectives:

  • Provide a learning platform for new & existing users
  • Develop different ideas for software that can help the society
  • Contribute to increasing the safety & security of IT solutions
  • Reduce the gap between the virtual and the online world
